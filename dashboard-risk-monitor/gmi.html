Broader macro assets (gold, copper, oil, yields, the dollar) that inform the overall macro picture but are not necessarily GMI positions.
The core value of GMI research is anticipating turns in the global liquidity cycle and the business cycle in advance, using our proprietary macro framework. GMI is a full-cycle investor: we position for the dominant trend and hold the best assets for the majority of the cycle, rather than trading moves within it. The weekly trend indicators above help you manage your own volatility tolerance around our strategic positioning.

Global M2 composite (47-economy, USD) is the GMI liquidity pulse; business cycle status is derived from the ISM Manufacturing PMI using the GMI monthly trend model. Weekly trend is the GMI proprietary volatility-scaled band signal; Secular Trend uses log regression channels for crypto, crypto-adjacent equities and EU carbon, and a 60-month SMA for traditional assets. EU Carbon (EUA) sourced via WisdomTree Carbon ETC (CARB.L). 30-day realised volatility (annualised) drives the risk colour: Normal <20% / Risky 20–40% / High >40%. Weekly asset trends are not intended for intraday decisions.
